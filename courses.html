Research Focus & Goals

I am an undergraduate student in Engineering Physics with a strong interest in cosmology, quantum mechanics, and astrophysical computation. My goal is to pursue graduate research that bridges theoretical and computational physics — modeling complex systems governed by quantum and gravitational dynamics. My current background includes AMO and optics laboratory work, vacuum science, and computational programming for physical system visualization.
