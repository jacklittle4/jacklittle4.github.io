Planetary science · Active

16 Psyche Topological Surface Mapping

With Dr. Shepard · Commonwealth University of Pennsylvania

Working with Dr. Shepard to computationally map the topological surface of asteroid 16 Psyche onto its radar-derived 3D shape model. The project involves spatial reasoning across non-Euclidean surface geometries and the reconciliation of multi-resolution observational data.

16 Psyche is one of the most massive metallic asteroids in the solar system and the target of NASA's Psyche mission. Accurate surface topographic mapping informs both compositional analysis and mission planning.

Exoplanet atmospheres · Completed

WASP-39b Transmission Spectrum Analysis

Independent · HST/WFC3 NIR data

Independent Python analysis of the near-infrared transmission spectrum of hot Jupiter WASP-39b using publicly available Hubble Space Telescope WFC3 data. The study focused on identifying and characterising the dominant water vapour absorption feature.

Results

~1.4 μm

H₂O absorption feature centre — reproduced from raw data

~900 km

Derived atmospheric scale height

~1500 K

Inferred equilibrium temperature

Python

NumPy · SciPy · Matplotlib pipeline

The analysis pipeline included limb-darkening correction, systematic noise removal, and transit depth extraction across the WFC3 G141 grism bandpass. Results are consistent with published literature values from Sing et al. and the JWST Early Release Science programme.

Atomic physics · Laboratory

Rydberg Laser Spectroscopy

Laser Laboratory · Commonwealth University of Pennsylvania

Laboratory work with tuneable laser systems probing Rydberg atomic states — highly excited states with principal quantum numbers n ≫ 1. These states exhibit exaggerated electromagnetic properties and are of interest in quantum information and precision measurement.

Work involved optical alignment, data acquisition, and spectroscopic analysis of transition frequencies across Rydberg series.
