Working with Dr. Shepard to computationally map topological surface features of asteroid 16 Psyche onto its radar-derived 3D shape model.

The project involves spatial reasoning across irregular surface geometry and reconciliation of observational data with a mission-relevant shape model.

Exoplanet atmospheres - completed

WASP-39b Transmission Spectrum Analysis

Independent Python analysis of the near-infrared transmission spectrum of hot Jupiter WASP-39b using publicly available HST/WFC3 data.

The analysis reproduced the dominant H2O absorption feature and estimated atmospheric quantities using a NumPy, SciPy, and Matplotlib pipeline.
