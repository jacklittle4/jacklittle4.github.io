Design notes

What each lab demonstrates.

The goal is not just decoration. The interface should show that the math, numerical model, and rendering are all connected.

Numerics

Stable algorithms

RK4, velocity Verlet, direct summation, and explicit ODE comparisons make the computation visible.

Visualization

Readable canvas layers

Axes, trails, grids, and live readouts keep the visuals useful instead of purely decorative.

Interaction

Adjustable models

Controls are tuned for quick exploration without hiding the physics behind the interface.
