Superposition

Adding amplitudes

Each source emits a circular wave. The field value at every point is the sum of all source amplitudes, so peaks can reinforce or cancel.

Interference

Phase differences

When two waves arrive in phase, the pattern brightens. When they arrive out of phase, the field crosses through a node and forms a dark interference band.
