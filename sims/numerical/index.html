Steps
Euler

First-order estimate

Euler follows the local slope forward. It is intuitive and fast, but error grows quickly when the curve bends.

Midpoint

Second-order correction

The midpoint method samples a slope halfway through the step, improving curvature response with little extra work.

RK4

Fourth-order workhorse

RK4 blends four slope estimates per step, often giving strong accuracy without tiny step sizes.
