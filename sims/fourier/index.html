From waveform to spectrum

A waveform shows amplitude over time. The Fourier transform rewrites that signal as frequency content, revealing fundamentals, harmonics, noise, and timbre.

X(f) = integral x(t) e^(-2*pi*i*f*t) dt
Resolution

FFT size tradeoff

Larger FFT sizes produce finer frequency bins but react more slowly. Smoothing makes the plot calmer, while low smoothing is better for fast transients.
