Biot-Savart

Direction wraps around current

For a straight wire pointing out of the screen, the magnetic field circulates by the right-hand rule. Reversing current reverses the arrows and field-line direction.

Superposition

Fields add as vectors

Multiple wires combine by vector addition. Opposite currents form a saddle-like pattern; same currents create reinforcing loops between conductors.
