Geometry

Schwarzschild metric

The simulation assumes a non-rotating, uncharged black hole. The event horizon sits at r = 2M, the photon sphere at r = 3M, and the innermost stable circular orbit for massive particles at r = 6M.

ds^2 = -(1 - 2M/r)dt^2 + (1 - 2M/r)^-1 dr^2 + r^2 dOmega^2
Integrator

Effective potential

Paths are integrated from the radial effective-potential equations using RK4. The step size is reduced near the horizon to keep the plotted trajectory stable.

(dr/dlambda)^2 = E^2 - (1 - 2M/r)(L^2/r^2 + epsilon)
